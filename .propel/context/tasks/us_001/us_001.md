# User Story: US-001 - Patient Appointment Booking

**Story ID:** US-001  
**Epic:** EP-001 (Patient Experience)  
**Created:** April 17, 2026  
**Status:** Draft  
**Priority:** High  
**Effort:** 8 story points  

---

## User Story

**As a** patient,  
**I want to** book an appointment with a healthcare provider,  
**So that** I can schedule my medical visit at a convenient time and location.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

### Functional Requirements
- [ ] Patient can search for available providers by specialty, location, and date range
- [ ] System displays real-time availability with provider credentials and ratings
- [ ] Patient can select an available time slot and proceed to booking review
- [ ] Booking review shows appointment details, provider info, and confirmation options
- [ ] Patient receives immediate confirmation with appointment details and reminders
- [ ] System reserves slot for 15 minutes during booking process
- [ ] Booking is blocked if patient has conflicting appointment within 24 hours

### Non-Functional Requirements
- [ ] Search results load within 1 second for up to 10K appointments
- [ ] Booking process completes within 3 steps maximum
- [ ] Confirmation SMS/email sent within 2 minutes of booking
- [ ] System handles 5,000 concurrent users during peak hours

### Edge Cases
- [ ] Slot becomes unavailable during booking process
- [ ] Patient attempts to book outside business hours
- [ ] Provider location is at capacity for selected time
- [ ] Patient has outstanding balance preventing booking

## Testing Notes

### Happy Path
1. Patient logs in to app/website
2. Searches for "Cardiology" in "Downtown Clinic"
3. Selects Dr. Smith, 2:00 PM slot on Tuesday
4. Reviews appointment details and confirms
5. Receives confirmation email and SMS

### Edge Case Testing
- Test booking when slot becomes unavailable mid-process
- Verify 24-hour conflict prevention
